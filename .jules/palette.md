## 2026-01-21 - Standardizing Entry Points for DX
**Learning:** Extremely long and complex filenames (e.g., `# Theoria...`) create significant friction for developers in terminal environments, impeding accessibility and file operations.
**Action:** Always sanitize filenames to kebab-case or standard naming conventions (like `DESIGN.md`) and ensure a `README.md` exists as a clear entry point for navigation.
